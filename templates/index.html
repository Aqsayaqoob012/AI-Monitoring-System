Tips for Effective Live Detection

Camera Positioning

Place your webcam at eye level for clear face and upper body detection. Avoid tilting the camera.

Lighting

Ensure your face and workspace are well-lit. Avoid strong backlight or shadows that can block detection.

Privacy

Keep your surroundings free from sensitive personal items. Make sure no other people appear in the camera view.

Stable Background

A simple, uncluttered background helps the system detect movements and objects accurately.

Stay Centered

Remain in the center of the camera frame for uninterrupted monitoring.
